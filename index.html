About Us

QA Trends is a full‑service quality assurance and software testing company. We partner with organizations across life sciences, banking and other regulated industries to ensure their digital products are reliable, secure and compliant. Our team comprises seasoned QA engineers, domain experts and consultants who provide end‑to‑end testing services, leadership consulting, training and team formation solutions.

Our Vision

To be recognised globally as the trusted partner for quality assurance and testing services. We strive to lead the industry through innovation, deep domain expertise and a relentless focus on customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower organisations to build dependable software by delivering comprehensive testing solutions, nurturing QA leadership and training the next generation of quality engineers. We aim to accelerate digital transformation while ensuring security, performance and compliance.

Our Services

Software Testing

We provide a complete suite of manual and automated testing. Our functional testing ensures applications meet their requirements by verifying behaviour against specifications. Non‑functional testing evaluates performance, scalability and usability to ensure the system behaves under real‑world conditions. Performance and load testing validate speed, stability and capacity, while security testing uncovers vulnerabilities and protects your data. Our automation experts use industry‑leading tools to write scripts and run test cases efficiently.

QA Leadership Consulting

We help organisations build robust quality strategies and frameworks. Our consultants assess your current processes, implement best‑practice methodologies, mentor QA leaders and guide you through digital transformation. Whether you need to adopt agile testing, implement DevOps or improve regulatory compliance, our experts will chart the right course.

QA Training & Internship

Our training programs bridge the gap between academia and industry. We offer courses on testing fundamentals, automation, performance and security as well as internships that expose trainees to real‑world projects. Through hands‑on learning, mentorship and assessment, we cultivate skilled QA professionals ready to contribute on day one.

Team Hiring & Formation

Building a high‑performing QA team takes time and expertise. We recruit, vet and onboard talent tailored to your needs—from senior test architects to entry‑level analysts. Our flexible staffing solutions let you scale up or down quickly while maintaining quality and productivity.

Computer System Validation

For life sciences and other regulated sectors, we validate computer systems in accordance with regulatory guidelines such as GxP and 21 CFR Part 11. Our CSV experts develop validation plans, risk assessments and documentation to ensure systems are compliant and audit‑ready.

Domain Expertise

Quality assurance isn’t one‑size‑fits‑all. Our testers understand the unique requirements of life sciences, banking, healthcare and other complex domains. This domain knowledge enables us to design tests that address regulatory, compliance and user considerations specific to your industry.

Market Potential

The global software testing market is expanding rapidly as digital transformation accelerates. Analysts estimate that the market size reached approximately USD 55.6 billion in 2024 and will grow to USD 145.84 billion by 2037 with a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of over 7.7 percent. Another report valued the market at USD 51.8 billion in 2023 and projected a CAGR of more than 7 percent between 2024 and 2032. Drivers of this growth include the integration of AI and machine learning into testing tools, the widespread adoption of cloud infrastructure and the rising demand for reliable mobile applications. As businesses rely on digital platforms, comprehensive quality assurance becomes indispensable.
